The First Floor: Descriptive Statistics

  • Give clues to principle trends
  • Suggest hints and more detailed look
  • Caption, unit, axis: well labeled
  • Statistical term: well defined
  • Measure of Location
    • Define the middle
      • Arithmetic mean
      • Median (insensitive to extreme values)
      • Mode
      • Geometric mean (serial dilution)
        log(y) = (1/n)Σ(log(xi)), i=1,2...n
  • Measure of Spread
    • Range (largest-smallest)
    • Quantiles
    • Variance and Standard Deviation
      • Mean Deviation: Σ(|xi - x̄|/n)
      • Sample Variance: Σ((xi - x̄)2)/(n-1)
      • Sample Standard Deviation: √(Sample Variance)
    • Coefficient of Variation: (s/x̄)×100%
  • Grouped Data
  • Graphic Methods
    • Bar Graph
    • Stem-and-Leaf Plot
    • Box Plot (inspect the skewness and outliers)
      • Extreme Outliers: upper + 3×IQR or lower - 3×IQR
